About Me

Hi there! My name is Shawn and I am a 24 year old college student studying Information Technology at NJIT. I was born in Philly and raised in South Jersey. I am excited to be teaching this class on Web Development with RWC, as I have a lot of experiance in this field. I'm also super happy to be able to give back, as when I was in middle and high school, I also participated in similar programs to RWC. I love teaching and programming, so choosing to teach this track was a no brainer.

Throughout my life, I have been involved in various technology-related activities. When I was in high school, I was the programming captain of our FIRST robotics team, which made it to World Championships and placed 7th in the world. When I made it to college, I became a member of NJIT's cybersecurity club, and also represented NJIT at MIT's Hackathon, where my team and I placed 11th out of 80 teams. Besides academia, I also have found ways to incorporate my tech skills at work. I've worked for Best Buy since I was 16, and spent a long time being a Repair Agent for the Geek Squad before I was promoted to a manager.

Programming has always been a passion of mine, ever since I was 11 years old. I built my first website when I was 13 and have been fascinated with web development ever since. Additionally, I have been a gamer practically my whole life and follow the video game scene very closely and spent a couple of years as a critic for new games that were coming out.

As a senior at NJIT, I am excited to graduate and get involved in the digital forensics field. In the long-term, I hope to attend law school as I believe our legal system needs to reflect the modern technology that makes up our everyday lives.

I hope this introduction gives you a better idea of who I am and what I am passionate about. I look forward to working with you all and helping you learn more about web development.
